Animation

This movie contains an amazing style of animation with awesome battle scenes. The animators play with both the color palette used in a variety of settings to represent the emotions of the characters and the animation styles. Each character, setting (dimension) has its own unique animation styles. Some characters took up to 2 years to animate since they were completely animated by hand with drawings

In this image you can see how different environments and characters have their own animation style, but this is not all, even the same character has different styles during the movie. Each dimencion has their unique set of styles, for example, in the dimention of Spider-Gwen you can see an amazing use of colors to demostrate emotions, which shows up incredible scenense, of fights and afection
